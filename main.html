    • About

    Virtual version of a game that was popular at parties in the beggining of 20th century, that was also a foundation for Alan Turing's famous test.

    Three players. One of them asks questions in the chat to find out other players genders. Two other players must have different genders (one man and one woman).

    Player A's role is to trick the interrogator into making the wrong decision, while player B attempts to assist the interrogator in making the right one. Player A and B can see the video chat at all times while interrogator can see the chat only after he made the final choice.

    After the game you can talk in the video-chat, discuss the game and exchange contacts)

    • How to play

    In this game you can find new friends or maybe even true love.

    There are 3 players and one of them is you.

    If you got a guessing role, you don't see the remaining two players until the end of the game. You need to ask questions to guess their gender. One of them is a man, and another is a woman.

    If you've got an imitator role, it is necessary to deceive the guessing one. You have to answer in such a manner so that he will guess your gender incorrectly.

    If your role is to tell the truth, your purpose is to help the guessing one to guess correctly about your gender and the gender of the imitator.
