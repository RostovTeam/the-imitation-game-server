    • About

    It’s a virtual version of the game that was popular at parties in the beginning of the 20th century. It also was the foundation for Alan Turing's famous test. Remember the movie? ;)

    Three players take part. One of them asks questions in the chat to find out other player’s genders. Two other players must have different genders (one man and one woman).

    Player A's role is to trick the interrogator into making the wrong decision, while player B attempts to assist the interrogator in making the right one. Player A and B can see the video chat at all times while interrogator can see the chat only after he’s made the final choice.

    After the game you can talk in the video-chat, discuss the game and exchange contacts :)
